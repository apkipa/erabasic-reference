**Summary**
- Stops all currently playing sound effects (all 10 sound effect slots).

**Tags**
- io

**Syntax**
- `STOPSOUND`

**Arguments**
- None.

**Semantics**
- Stops playback of all sound effect slots (`0..9`).
- Does not affect BGM (`PLAYBGM`).

**Errors & validation**
- (none)

**Examples**
- `STOPSOUND`

**Progress state**
- complete
